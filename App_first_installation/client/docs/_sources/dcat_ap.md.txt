# The DCAT-AP_IT Standard

Selecting the DCAT-AP_IT item from the Menu leads to the section dedicated to the compilation/publication of Metadata according to the DCAT-AP_IT standard.

 ![METADATASET](_static/img/dcat_lista_metadataset.PNG "METADATASET")
 
The standard requires the compilation of Metadata inherent to the Catalogue and Metadata inherent to the single published Dataflow.
